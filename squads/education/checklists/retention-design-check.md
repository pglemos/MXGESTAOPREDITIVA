# Checklist: Retention Design Check

**Version:** 1.0
**Agent:** bjork-engineer
**When to use:** During curriculum review or practice design
**Pass threshold:** 5/6 items minimum

---

## Items

- [ ] **Spacing used** — Content revisited across multiple sessions | PASS criteria: Key concepts appear in at least 3 separate lessons with increasing intervals
- [ ] **Interleaving present** — Mixed practice of different concepts, not blocked | PASS criteria: Practice mixes concepts A/B/C, not A-A-A then B-B-B
- [ ] **Retrieval practice in every lesson** — Active recall required, not just re-reading | PASS criteria: Each lesson has quiz, self-test, or recall prompt
- [ ] **Variability in exercises** — Same concept practiced in different contexts | PASS criteria: Concept applied in at least 3 different scenarios/formats
- [ ] **No massed practice** — Practice distributed over time, not crammed | PASS criteria: No single practice session exceeds 30 minutes on same concept
- [ ] **Desirable difficulty appropriate** — Challenge level creates effortful retrieval | PASS criteria: Retrieval success rate 70-85% (not too easy, not too hard)

## Veto Conditions (auto-FAIL)
- No retrieval practice (only re-reading or passive review)
- Massed practice (all practice in one session)
- No spacing (concept taught once, never revisited)

## Scoring
- **PASS:** 5/6 or more
- **WARN:** 3-4/6 (some retention strategies present)
- **FAIL:** 2/6 or fewer
