# Checklist: MEC Curso Livre Check

**Version:** 1.0
**Agent:** mec-compliance
**When to use:** Before issuing certificates or marketing curso livre
**Pass threshold:** 4/6 items minimum

---

## Items

- [ ] **Certificate states "curso livre" clearly** — Explicit "curso livre" designation | PASS criteria: Certificate contains "Curso Livre" prominently
- [ ] **No MEC logo or seal used** — No government symbols/seals | PASS criteria: Zero MEC, government, or official-looking seals
- [ ] **Learning outcomes described** — What learner will be able to do after course | PASS criteria: Outcomes list in course description or certificate
- [ ] **Hours documented** — Total course hours stated clearly | PASS criteria: Workload (carga horária) specified
- [ ] **Outcomes scoped to course hours** — Learning outcomes complexity is proportional to course workload (8h course ≠ graduation-level outcomes) | PASS criteria: Outcomes are realistic for declared hours
- [ ] **No misleading equivalence claims** — Certificate and marketing materials do NOT compare or equate the curso livre to formal education (técnico, graduação, pós) | PASS criteria: Zero equivalence claims found | VETO: Any claim of MEC recognition or formal equivalence

## Veto Conditions (auto-FAIL)
- MEC logo or seal present
- Certificate implies government recognition
- Curso livre marketed as equivalent to formal education

## Scoring
- **PASS:** 4/6 or more
- **WARN:** 3/6 (minor compliance issues)
- **FAIL:** 2/6 or fewer
