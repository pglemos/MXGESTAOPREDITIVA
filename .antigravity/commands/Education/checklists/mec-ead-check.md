# Checklist: MEC EaD Check

> ⚠️ **REGULATORY NOTICE**: Verify all decreto/lei references against current MEC regulations before use. Legislation may have been updated. Last verified: 2026-02.

**Version:** 1.0
**Agent:** mec-compliance
**When to use:** Before offering distance education course
**Pass threshold:** 6/7 items minimum

---

## Items

- [ ] **Decreto 12.456/2025 compliant** — Follows new EaD regulations | PASS criteria: Course design references and follows Decreto 12.456/2025
- [ ] **Format correct** — Presencial/semi-presencial/EaD ratios correct | PASS criteria: If semi-presencial, presencial component 20-80%; if EaD, assessments presencial
- [ ] **Area restrictions checked** — Course not in restricted areas (health clinical, engineering labs, etc.) | PASS criteria: Area checked against MEC restricted list
- [ ] **Assessments presencial** — Final/summative assessments presencial | PASS criteria: Presencial assessment locations and schedule defined
- [ ] **Minimum 10 weeks per UC** — Each unidade curricular at least 10 weeks duration | PASS criteria: No UC shorter than 10 weeks
- [ ] **Polo infrastructure** — Polos have required infrastructure (library, labs if needed, accessibility) | PASS criteria: Each polo has infrastructure report
- [ ] **Credenciamento EaD** — Institution credencialed for EaD | PASS criteria: Institution has credenciamento EaD in e-MEC

## Veto Conditions (auto-FAIL)
- Course in restricted area without MEC exception
- No presencial assessments
- Institution not credencialed for EaD
- UC shorter than 10 weeks

## Scoring
- **PASS:** 6/7 or more
- **WARN:** 4-5/7 (compliance issues present)
- **FAIL:** 3/7 or fewer
