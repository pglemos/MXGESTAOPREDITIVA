# Checklist: Multimedia Principles Check

**Version:** 1.0
**Agent:** mayer-presenter
**When to use:** During multimedia content production or review
**Pass threshold:** 9/12 items minimum

---

## Items

- [ ] **Coherence** — Extraneous words, pictures, sounds excluded | PASS criteria: No decorative elements, music, or irrelevant content
- [ ] **Signaling** — Essential material highlighted via cues | PASS criteria: Key points highlighted, bolded, or called out explicitly
- [ ] **Redundancy avoided** — Not animation + narration + on-screen text simultaneously | PASS criteria: Animation + narration OR animation + text, not all three
- [ ] **Spatial contiguity** — Corresponding words and pictures near each other | PASS criteria: Labels on or next to visuals, not separated
- [ ] **Temporal contiguity** — Corresponding words and pictures presented simultaneously | PASS criteria: Narration synchronized with relevant visual
- [ ] **Segmenting** — Learner-paced or broken into chunks | PASS criteria: Videos have pause points or are under 6 minutes
- [ ] **Pre-training** — Key concepts/vocabulary introduced before main lesson | PASS criteria: Glossary or concept primer before complex content
- [ ] **Modality** — Graphics explained with narration, not on-screen text | PASS criteria: Diagrams/animations have voice-over, not text blocks
- [ ] **Multimedia** — Words and pictures, not words alone | PASS criteria: Concepts illustrated with visuals, not text-only slides
- [ ] **Personalization** — Conversational style, not formal | PASS criteria: First/second person ("you", "we"), not third person
- [ ] **Voice** — Human voice narration, not machine | PASS criteria: Narration is human voice (not TTS unless high-quality)
- [ ] **Image** — No on-screen agent unless pedagogically necessary | PASS criteria: Talking head/avatar absent OR justified by social presence research

## Veto Conditions (auto-FAIL)
- Redundancy violation (animation + narration + identical on-screen text)
- Split-attention violation (labels far from visuals)
- Machine voice when human voice available

## Scoring
- **PASS:** 9/12 or more
- **WARN:** 7-8/12 (some violations but functional)
- **FAIL:** 6/12 or fewer
