# Checklist: MEC Pós-Graduação Lato Sensu Check

**Version:** 1.0
**Agent:** mec-compliance
**When to use:** Before offering specialization course
**Pass threshold:** 5/7 items minimum

---

## Items

- [ ] **360h minimum** — Course has at least 360 hours (Resolução CNE/CES 1/2018) | PASS criteria: Total workload ≥ 360h
- [ ] **30% masters/doctors** — At least 30% of faculty have mestrado or doutorado | PASS criteria: Faculty list shows ≥30% with advanced degrees
- [ ] **PPC present** — Projeto Pedagógico de Curso exists | PASS criteria: PPC document with objectives, curriculum, faculty, methodology
- [ ] **Specialist certificate format correct** — Certificate says "Especialista em..." | PASS criteria: Certificate template uses "Curso de Especialização" and "Especialista"
- [ ] **Institution authorized** — IES authorized to offer pós lato sensu | PASS criteria: Institution has credenciamento or recredenciamento
- [ ] **Assessments aligned to learning outcomes** — Each module has assessments at LTEM tier 5+ (decision-making or task performance) | PASS criteria: Assessment matrix shows tier 5+ for critical modules
- [ ] **Capstone/final project defined** — If specialization requires monografia or TCC, requirements are documented (scope, format, evaluation criteria, advisor assignment) | PASS criteria: Capstone brief exists with clear criteria | N/A: If program structure doesn't require capstone

## Veto Conditions (auto-FAIL)
- Below 360 hours
- Faculty qualification below 30% masters/doctors
- Institution not authorized

## Scoring
- **PASS:** 5/7 or more
- **WARN:** 4/7 (minor issues)
- **FAIL:** 3/7 or fewer
