# Track Specification: Legacy to MX Performance Migration

## Overview
Full absorption of Google Apps Script (GAS) legacy logic into the MX Performance system.

## Scope
1.  **Core Data**: Canonical model (DATA, LOJA, VENDEDOR, LEADS, VND_PORTA, AGD_CART, VND_CART, AGD_NET, VND_NET, VISITA).
2.  **Import**: Aggressive/resilient importer (mapping, normalisation, deduplication).
3.  **Config**: Store-level governance (meta, contacts, benchmarks).
4.  **Dashboard**: Executive view + operational grid.
5.  **Manager Routine**: Launch tracking, accountability, ranking.
6.  **Automation**: Morning report, Weekly feedback, Monthly close.
7.  **Admin**: Global vision, semaphores, projection.
8.  **Communication**: WhatsApp/Email broadcast engine.
9.  **Intelligence**: Bottleneck diagnosis and training suggestions.
10. **Operations**: Scheduling, Idempotency, Logs.

## Rules of Business
- D-1/D-0 logic.
- Benchmarks 20/60/33.
- Meta individual (with VENDA LOJA exclusion logic).
- Pacing-based monthly projection.
